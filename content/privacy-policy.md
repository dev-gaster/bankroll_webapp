# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** September 25, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Bankroll ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Service"). Bankroll is a fundraising platform that allows users to create and manage fundraising campaigns for their events and participate in community features.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

- **Register for an account:** Name, email address, phone number, and profile information
- **Create fundraising campaigns:** Campaign title, description, fundraising goals, event details, images, and related content
- **Make pledges or donations:** Payment information, pledge amounts, and transaction details
- **Join communities:** Community preferences, interests, and participation data
- **Contact us:** Messages, feedback, and support requests

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Service, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
- **Usage Data:** App usage patterns, features accessed, time spent on the app, and interaction data
- **Location Information:** General location data (if you grant permission) to show relevant campaigns and communities
- **Log Information:** IP address, access times, pages viewed, and app crashes

### 2.3 Information from Third-Party Sources

We may collect information from third-party sources, including:

- **Social Media:** If you connect your social media accounts, we may access profile information, contacts, and other data you authorize
- **Payment Providers:** Transaction information and payment verification data
- **Analytics Services:** Usage statistics and performance metrics

### 2.4 Contacts Access

With your explicit permission, we may access your device contacts to:

- Help you find friends who are already using Bankroll
- Suggest people you might want to invite to your campaigns
- Facilitate sharing of campaigns with your network
- Enable community invitations and connections

**Note:** We only access contacts with your explicit consent, and you can revoke this permission at any time through your device settings.

### 3.3 Communication and Marketing

- Send promotional communications about new features (with your consent)

- Share updates about successful campaigns and community achievements
- Provide personalized content and recommendations

### 3.4 Legal and Safety

- Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
- Protect against fraudulent, unauthorized, or illegal activity
- Enforce our Terms of Service and other policies
- Resolve disputes and legal claims

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Public Information

The following information may be publicly visible on our platform:

- Campaign details, descriptions, and images you choose to make public
- Your public profile information (name, profile picture, bio)
- Community posts and interactions you make publicly
- Pledge amounts and supporter comments (if you choose to make them public)

### 4.2 With Other Users

We may share certain information with other users:

- Campaign creators can see supporter information for their campaigns
- Community members can see other members' profiles and activities within the community
- Users you invite may see your name and profile information

### 4.3 With Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our Service:

- **Payment Processors:** To handle transactions and pledges securely
- **Cloud Storage Providers:** To store and manage data
- **Analytics Services:** To understand app usage and improve performance
- **Customer Support Tools:** To provide efficient customer service
- **Marketing Platforms:** To send communications (with your consent)

### 4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in good faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to:

- Comply with legal process or government requests
- Protect and defend our rights or property
- Investigate potential violations of our Terms of Service
- Protect the safety of users or the public
- Respond to emergencies or urgent situations

### 4.5 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

- **Encryption:** Data transmission and storage encryption
- **Access Controls:** Restricted access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Security Audits:** Ongoing assessment of our security practices
- **Secure Payment Processing:** PCI DSS compliant payment handling
- **Employee Training:** Regular privacy and security training for our team

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our Service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Specific retention periods include:

- **Account Information:** Until you delete your account or request deletion
- **Campaign Data:** For the duration of the campaign plus 7 years for financial record-keeping
- **Transaction Records:** 7 years for tax and legal compliance purposes
- **Support Communications:** 3 years for quality assurance and legal purposes
- **Usage Analytics:** Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely

You may request deletion of your personal information at any time, subject to legal and operational requirements.
